RUSKIN'S CAFE

We are here for your coffee breaks! Since 2015.

All about us!

Ruskin's Cafe was found in 2015 in the loving memory of Ruskin Doe. He was a cheerful 12 year old who loved having croissants and pizzas. We aim to provide you a lovely ambience and amazing coffee with snacks so you can make full out of your coffee breaks. We also have a community support dog Marley who is out here to help you whenever you feel a bit down. Feel free to pet him even if you are in your happiest spirits!

1334 Citadel St. Halifax
